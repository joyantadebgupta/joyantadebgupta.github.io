About Me

A brief introduction to my professional journey and passion.

I am a seasoned Electrical Design & Project Engineer, bringing over nine years of comprehensive experience in the dynamic field of electrical engineering. My expertise spans from medium-voltage (33kV) down to low-voltage (LV) system design, encompassing intricate substation design (both AIS & GIS), meticulous Single Line Diagrams (SLD), Electrical Layout Diagrams (ELD), robust Earthing System Design, and efficient Busbar Trunking (BBT) design.

My approach is rooted in thorough site data collection and a keen understanding of client requirements, enabling me to craft customized and effective electrical solutions. I am proficient in electrical calculations, detailed Bill of Quantities (BOQ) preparation, precise cost estimation, and strategic sourcing for diverse projects.

Beyond design, I am deeply involved in project execution, diligently overseeing implementation, rigorous testing, and final commissioning. My commitment is to ensure every project not only meets but exceeds industry standards and safety regulations, delivering efficient, cost-effective, and high-quality outcomes through strong technical coordination and project management.
