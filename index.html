About Me

A brief introduction to my professional journey and passion.

I am an accomplished Electrical Design & Project Engineer with over 9.5 years of comprehensive experience in the dynamic field of electrical engineering. My expertise spans from medium-voltage (33kV) utility-scale substations to industrial power systems and compliance-driven factory electrification in Bangladesh.

I have a proven track record in leading 50+ high-stakes projects—from tendering and detailed design to erection, testing, and commissioning. My work ensures 100% adherence to BNBC, NFPA, NESCO, DESCO, and major buyer standards like RSC, Nirapon, Elevate, and Bureau Veritas.

Recognized for technical leadership and zero-incident safety performance, I have delivered mission-critical assets including 33/11kV substations and 16–28 MVA power transformers, earning the trust of clients like NESCO, DESCO, ACI, Epic Group, and the Bangladesh National Parliament.
